About / Bio

I am a senior research scientist and tenure-track Assistant Professor at the Polytechnic University of Bari, Italy. I serve as Senior PC for major conferences such as SIGIR, CIKM, ECAI, and TheWebConf. Additionally, I am an Associate Editor of IEEE Transactions on Knowledge & Data Engineering (TKDE) and ACM Computing Surveys (CSUR). I also function as a guest editor for special issues in ACM Transactions on Recommender Systems (TORS): 2024 on Trustworthy Recommender Systems and 2025 on Generative Recommender Systems.

I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Politecnico di Milano, Italy, with distinction, and my M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from Chalmers University of Technology, Sweden. My research spans trustworthy recommender systems (fairness, robustness, explainability) and the emerging role of Generative AI & Large Language Models (LLMs) in Recommender Systems. My work also explores the interplay between multi-modal content (fashion, video, music) and data-driven personalization, focusing on the societal impact and ethical considerations of AI-driven technologies.

I am the lead editor of the forthcoming book Recommendation with Generative Models (Now Publishers "FnT IR", expected 2024-2025), building on the GenRecSys-Survey and my tutorial at KDD’24. I have authored key chapters in Recommender Systems Handbook (2021) and co-authored several survey articles (e.g., on Fashion RecSys, Music RecSys, Fairness, Interactive QA) in top-tier venues (ACM CSUR, UMUAI, and more).

In 2023 and 2024, I was ranked among the top 2% of research scientists globally by Stanford University’s Ioannidis database.
