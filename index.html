About Me

Yashar Deldjoo is a highly accomplished senior researcher and tenure‐track Assistant Professor at the Polytechnic University of Bari, Italy. With a Ph.D. from Politecnico di Milano and an M.Sc. from Chalmers University of Technology, his work has consistently set new benchmarks in the field of recommender systems.

His research focuses on developing trustworthy recommender systems that seamlessly integrate fairness, adversarial robustness, and explainability, as well as pioneering the use of Generative AI and large language models in personalization. His recent contributions include influential surveys, innovative frameworks, and state‐of‐the‐art methodologies that have had a significant impact both academically and in practice.

Yashar currently serves as an Associate Editor for IEEE TKDE and ACM Computing Surveys (CSUR), in addition to acting as a Distinguished Reviewer for ACM TORS. He is the Guest Editor for two special issues—one on Generative Recommender Systems (2025) and another on Trustworthy Recommender Systems (2024).

Recognized among the top 2% of global research scientists, his work continues to influence both academic inquiry and real-world applications.
