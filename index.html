About Me

Yashar Deldjoo is a senior research scientist and a tenure-track Assistant Professor at the Polytechnic University of Bari, Italy. He is passionate about trustworthy recommender systems and the integration of Generative AI in personalization. His research focuses on fairness, adversarial robustness, explainability, and the responsible use of large language models in recommender systems.

Currently, Yashar serves as an Associate Editor for IEEE TKDE and ACM Computing Surveys (CSUR). He is also a Distinguished Reviewer for ACM Transaction on Recommender Systems (TORS) and the Guest Editor for two special issues: one on Generative Recommender Systems (2025) and another on Trustworthy Recommender Systems (2024).

With a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Politecnico di Milano (Italy) and an M.Sc. from Chalmers University of Technology (Sweden), his work has been recognized among the top 2% of research scientists worldwide.
