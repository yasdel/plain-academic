Dr. Yashar Deldjoo

Senior Research Scientist & Assistant Professor in Trustworthy AI and Generative Recommender Systems

About Me

I am a tenure-track Assistant Professor at the Polytechnic University of Bari, Italy, and a senior research scientist working at the nexus of Trustworthy AI and Generative Recommender Systems. Recognized among the top 2% of research scientists worldwide (per Stanford University's Ioannidis database), my work focuses on incorporating fairness, adversarial robustness, and explainability into next-generation recommendation frameworks.

I serve as an Associate Editor for IEEE TKDE and ACM Computing Surveys (CSUR), and as a Distinguished Reviewer for ACM Transactions on Recommender Systems (TORS). I am the Guest Editor for two TORS special issues on Generative RecSys (2025) and Trustworthy RecSys (2024). My recent endeavors include influential surveys, pioneering technical contributions, and initiatives examining the societal impacts of large language models in recommendation.
