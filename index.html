My name is Yashar Deldjoo. I am a tenure-track assistant professor at Polytechnic University of Bari, Italy. My research focuses on applying principles of trustworthy AI, such as explainability and fairness, as well as adversarial robustness and prviacy to recommender system and machine learning. I have recently visited UCSD, USA, contributing to research on simulation validation and inconsistency of RS performance ranking on real-world, and synthetic data.
I completed my PhD in 2018 at the Polytechnic University of Milan, Italy, where I spent as a visting research period at Johannes Kepler University (JKU), Linz, Austria. The focus of my PhD research was on recommender systems leveraging multimedia content.
From 2017 to 2020, I organized a series of machine-learning competitions in collaboration with major industrial partners including Twitter, Xing, and Spotify. Prior to my PhD, I worked at multimedia department of Ericsson Research, (Kista) Stockholm, Sweden which led to a Master's degree from Chalmers Univeristy of Technology, Gothenburg, Sweden.
