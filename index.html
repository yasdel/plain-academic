Yashar Deldjoo is a senior research scientist and tenure-track Assistant Professor at the Polytechnic University of Bari, Italy. Renowned for his groundbreaking work in Trustworthy Recommender Systems and Generative AI, he has been recognized among the top 2% of research scientists worldwide in 2023 and 2024 (Stanford’s Ioannidis Database).

Yashar serves as Associate Editor for IEEE Transactions on Knowledge and Data Engineering (TKDE) and ACM Computing Surveys (CSUR). He is also a Distinguished Reviewer for ACM TORS, where he currently oversees two special issues: “Generative Recommender Systems (2025)” and “Trustworthy Recommender Systems (2024)”.

His research explores fairness, adversarial robustness, explainability, and the responsible adoption of Large Language Models in personalized recommendations. Yashar has championed new lines of research on multi-modal recommendation and spearheads the forthcoming book "Recommendation with Generative Models" based on his GenRecSys Survey (KDD'24). He has served as Senior Program Committee member for SIGIR, CIKM, ECAI, and TheWebConf, and is regularly invited to speak at major AI/RecSys forums globally.
