About Me

I am a senior research scientist and a tenure-track Assistant Professor at the Polytechnic University of Bari, Italy, specializing in Trustworthy Recommender Systems and Generative AI. My work blends fairness, adversarial robustness, explainability, and the emerging potentials of generative models to shape the future of recommendation technology.

I am honored to serve as an Associate Editor for both IEEE TKDE and ACM Computing Surveys (CSUR) while actively contributing to leading conferences and workshops worldwide.
