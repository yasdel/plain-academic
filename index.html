About Me

Yashar Deldjoo is a highly accomplished senior research scientist and tenure-track Assistant Professor at the Polytechnic University of Bari, Italy. Recognized among the top 2% of research scientists worldwide, his work has significantly advanced the fields of recommender systems and trustworthy artificial intelligence.

As an Associate Editor for IEEE TKDE and ACM Computing Surveys (CSUR), and a Distinguished Reviewer for ACM Transaction on Recommender Systems (TORS), Yashar has spearheaded multiple high-impact initiatives. He is the Guest Editor for two prestigious special issues: one focusing on Generative Recommender Systems (2025) and another on Trustworthy Recommender Systems (2024).

His research integrates state-of-the-art generative models and large language models with recommender system design, addressing challenges of fairness, robustness, and explainability. Recent outcomes include influential surveys, breakthrough technical contributions, and innovative tutorials that have reshaped personalization in domains such as healthcare, finance, and entertainment.
