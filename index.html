About Me

I am a senior research scientist and tenure-track Assistant Professor at the Polytechnic University of Bari, Italy. My work is driven by a passion for advancing the theory and practice of recommender systems through rigorous research in trustworthy artificial intelligence and generative models.

Recognized among the top 2% of research scientists globally, I have built a career on a strong foundation of empirical research and real‐world impact. I serve as an Associate Editor for IEEE TKDE and ACM Computing Surveys (CSUR) and contribute as a Distinguished Reviewer for ACM Transaction on Recommender Systems (TORS). I also lead two special issues focusing on Generative Recommender Systems (2025) and Trustworthy Recommender Systems (2024).

My research spans the development of advanced recommender systems integrating fairness, robustness, and explainability with state-of-the-art generative AI and large language models. My recent outcomes include influential surveys, innovative technical contributions, and comprehensive tutorials that have informed both academic inquiry and industry practice.
