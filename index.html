Dr. Yashar Deldjoo
Senior Researcher & Assistant Professor, Polytechnic University of Bari

Dr. Yashar Deldjoo is a tenure-track Assistant Professor in Computer Science at the Polytechnic University of Bari, Italy, where he focuses on Trustworthy AI and Generative Recommender Systems. Recognized among the top 2% of research scientists worldwide (Stanford’s Ioannidis database), his work examines how fairness, adversarial robustness, and explainability can be integrated into next-generation recommender systems. Dr. Deldjoo currently serves as an Associate Editor for IEEE TKDE and ACM Computing Surveys (CSUR), and is a Distinguished Reviewer for ACM Transactions on Recommender Systems (TORS). He is also the Guest Editor for upcoming TORS special issues on Generative RecSys (2025) and Trustworthy RecSys (2024). His broader research interests encompass data-driven personalization, large language models, and the socio-technical impacts of AI.

Research

My research centers on designing and evaluating reliable and trustworthy Artificial Intelligence (AI) models, with a special emphasis on:

  • Adversarial robustness
  • Fairness & equity in recommendations
  • Explainability and interpretability of AI systems
  • Leveraging LLMs for generative personalization

I seek to bridge theoretical rigor with real-world applicability, ensuring AI systems remain both innovative and socially responsible.
